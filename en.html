But what is La Base
Sports Clinic?

Initially propelled by the dream of two podiatrists to settle their little family in Bromont, La Base has come to life on its own to become a community of health professionals all working towards the same goal: allow you to play more outside!

Ski Simulator: Bootfitting and Training

The Base has also become an unusual place to practice skiing... at the base of the mountain! The population of the surroundings now have at their disposal a high-tech ski simulator allowing them to have personalized support from a ski professional.

Out of the ordinary podiatric clinic

From the occasional cyclist to the passionate golfer through the young skier, our two podiatrists always work towards helping you practice your activities in joy . Because let's be honest, there is little room for pleasure when your feet hurt!

Ski Simulator

A ski simula-what?? It is a high-tech equipment that reproduces the centrifugal force at each skier's turns, which simulates the sensations of a real run on the slopes.
This technology can be adapted to both beginners and elite skiers and allows them to quickly improve their technique, balance, endurance and reaction speed during training.
