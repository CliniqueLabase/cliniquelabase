But what is La Base Sports Clinic?

Initially propelled by the dream of two podiatrists to settle their small family in Bromont, La Base has come to life on its own to become a community of health professionals all working towards the same goal: to help you play better outside!

Ski Simulator: Bootfitting and Training

The Base has also become an unusual place to practice skiing... at the base of the mountain! The people of the region now have at their disposal a high-tech ski simulator allowing them to have personalized support from a ski professional.

Out of the ordinary podiatric clinic

From the occasional cyclist to the passionate golfer through the young skier, our two podiatrists always work with the aim of helping you practice your activities in happiness. Because we are going to tell each other, there is little room for pleasure when your feet hurt!

Ski Simulator

A ski simulation? It is a high-tech device that reproduces the centrifugal force at each of the skier's turns, which simulates the sensations of a real descent on the slopes.
This technology can be adapted to both beginners and elite skiers and allows them to quickly improve their technique, balance, endurance and reaction speed during training.
